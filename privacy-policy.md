## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not require you to create an account.
- We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information directly.
- Game progress is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted to our servers.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically via Third-Party Services

The App uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following information automatically:

- Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
- IP address
- Device type, operating system, and version
- Approximate location (derived from IP address)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
- App usage data for ad targeting and measurement

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy. We do not have direct access to this data.

## 3. How Information Is Used

We use the information collected through AdMob to:

- Display relevant advertisements within the App
- Measure ad performance
- Support the free operation of the App

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

## 5. Advertising and Ad Personalization

AdMob may use your advertising ID to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings:

- **iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
- **iOS (Limit Ad Tracking):** Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → disable "Personalized Ads"

---

## 6. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our servers. Game state data stored locally on your device is retained until you uninstall the App or clear app data.

Google's data retention practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through our App, please contact us at info@remedypointsolutions.com and we will take steps to remove such information.
